CANTERBURY

THE HOME OF THE CANTERBURY TALES

Why Canterbury, England?

Canterbury is the home of the Canterbury Cathedral, one of the oldest and most famous Christian structures in England. It is also the location of the death of Saint Thomas Becket, the Archbishop of Canterbury who was murdered in 1170, giving the location some history to look for.

If you like history, literature, or travelling, Canterbury is a great place because of its historic background, the Canterbury Tales were written during the pilgrimage to the Canterbury Cathedral to honor the death of Becket.
