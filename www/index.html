Whether you enjoy literature, history, or travelling, you'll enjoy Canterbury because of its rich culture, extensive history, and great spots to visit.

Canterbury is the home of the Canterbury Cathedral, one of the oldest and most famous Christian structures in England. It is also the location of the death of Saint Thomas Becket, the Archbishop of Canterbury who was murdered in 1170, giving the location some history to look for.

Canterbury was also the location of interest in the pilgrimage that the people interested in honoring his death. If you want, you could take the trip that they did from Winchester to Canterbury, also known as Pilgrims' Way, which is another flight and a whole other tour.
